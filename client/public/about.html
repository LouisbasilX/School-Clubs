About Hillrange Clubs

Discover our mission, history, and the team behind our vibrant club community.

Our Mission

At Hillrange School Clubs, we believe extracurricular activities are essential to holistic education. Our mission is to provide diverse opportunities for students to explore their passions, develop leadership skills, and build meaningful connections.

Passion Development

Help students discover and nurture their interests beyond the classroom.

Community Building

Create inclusive spaces where students form lasting friendships.

Our Journey

A brief history of how our club program evolved

Club Program Founded

2010

Started with just 5 clubs: Debate, Chess, Art, Science, and Music. 78 students participated in the first year.

First Inter-School Competition

2013

Our debate team won first place in the regional championships, putting Hillrange on the map.

STEM Expansion

2016

Added Robotics, Coding, and Engineering clubs with new lab facilities. Participation doubled.

Digital Platform Launch

2021

Introduced this online portal, making club management and signups accessible to all students.
